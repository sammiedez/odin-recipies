Sammie's Macaroni and Cheese with Eggs!

Description:

This dish is one created by me, which consists of Kraft Deluxe, and Eggs on top of it, served on a bowl. You can use any Macaroni and Cheese, however I chose to use Kraft as it's the most available.

Ingredients:

  • Kraft Macaroni and Cheese Deluxe (or any macaroni of your choosing)
  • 3 or 4 eggs
  • Any spices you like to enjoy on your eggs!

Steps:

  1. First, make your macaroni using either the steps on the box or whatever macaroni recipie you follow
  2. Then, prepare the eggs by scrambling them in a bowl
  3. Butter the pan, and let the butter heat up a little
  4. Pour the eggs on the pan, and cook until it just begins to solidify

Thats all!
